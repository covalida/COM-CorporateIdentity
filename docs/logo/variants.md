# Logo Variants

The COVALIDA logo comes in four production variants. They are one family — same Core-Shield, same Montserrat wordmark, same teal — tuned for different shapes and contexts. Pick the variant that fits the space, then set it on the right background (positive on light and mint, dark variant on dark).

## Horizontal — primary

The default lockup: symbol on the left, **COVALIDA** wordmark on the right. Use it almost everywhere — website headers, document footers, presentation title bars, email signatures, social banners, partner co-branding.

## Vertical — stacked

Symbol centered above the wordmark. Reach for it when the layout is **square or narrow** and the horizontal lockup would shrink too far — a centered cover slide, a social profile card, a sidebar, a portrait-format sponsor wall.

## Symbol — gradient

The Core-Shield on its own, with the full teal gradient. Use it wherever the name is already established or space is tight and square: **app icon, social avatar, favicon, watermark**, loading states, a large decorative element behind a headline.

<div class="logo-stage mint"><img src="/brand/covalida-icon.svg" alt="COVALIDA gradient symbol on mint" style="max-width:160px" /></div>

The gradient symbol holds up on light and mint and on quiet dark surfaces. Give it the same clear space as the full logo. File: [`covalida-icon.svg`](/brand/covalida-icon.svg)

## Flat mono — single color

A one-color version of the symbol in **Deep Teal `#008080`**, with no gradient. Use it where a gradient cannot be reproduced or would break down: **single-color printing constraints in third-party tools, engraving, embossing, stitched or etched contexts, and very small favicons** where gradient banding would muddy the shape.

<div class="logo-stage light"><img src="/brand/covalida-icon-flat.svg" alt="COVALIDA flat mono symbol in Deep Teal" style="max-width:160px" /></div>

The flat mono mark may also be rendered in solid white on a dark brand color, or in Deep Slate Gray where a more neutral treatment is needed — but never in an off-brand color. File: [`covalida-icon-flat.svg`](/brand/covalida-icon-flat.svg)

::: info Digital-first, always
COVALIDA is a 100% digital brand. "Engraving" or "single-color" contexts refer to real production constraints you may meet in partner tooling — not to a COVALIDA print program. There is no stationery, letterhead or physical merch to design for.
:::

## Decision guide

Work top to bottom — the first row that matches your context wins.

| Context | Use this variant | File |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Default — headers, footers, docs, slides, email | **Horizontal** | `covalida-logo-horizontal(.svg / -dark.svg)` |
| Square or narrow space that still needs the name | **Vertical** | `covalida-logo-vertical(.svg / -dark.svg)` |
| App icon, avatar, favicon, watermark | **Symbol — gradient** | `covalida-icon.svg` |
| Gradient can't render — engraving, tiny favicon, single-color tool | **Flat mono** | `covalida-icon-flat.svg` |
| Dark background (any variant) | Switch to the matching **dark / white** version | `…-dark.svg` |

## Choosing background and version

- **Light or Light Mint background** → positive logo (dark slate wordmark, gradient symbol).
- **Dark background** → dark variant (white wordmark, gradient symbol), or the flat mono symbol in white.
- **Busy or low-contrast background** → don't. Move the logo onto a calm panel first (see [Misuse](/logo/donts)).

## Get the files

Every variant is available as master **SVG** and high-resolution **PNG** on the [Downloads](/downloads/) page. Prefer SVG wherever the surface supports it — it stays crisp at any size. For construction, clear space and minimum sizes, see [Construction &amp; Clear Space](/logo/construction).
