## About this brand guide

COVALIDA is the holistic EU-compliance partner — the strategic protector of a company's core. This guide is the binding reference for everyone who designs for the brand: it defines the visual identity, explains the five business pillars, and hands designers the exact colors, type, icons and assets needed to produce clean, consistent, on-brand work across every digital surface.

<div class="note">
  <strong>Who this is for:</strong> designers, marketers and partners creating COVALIDA materials. Use the specifications and download the ready-made assets rather than recreating them. When in doubt, favor restraint — clarity and calm are core to the brand.
